Kevin Kimmett
Senior Product Designer

Over the past years, I have had the opportunity to develop my digital design craft into a wide range of disciplines. By gaining plenty of insight into how a user interacts with a product I am able to create approachable and user-friendly solutions.

As the Senior Product Designer for CBC's Native Apps & Voice Platforms team, my focus is on crafting user-centred experiences and interfaces for our products. Collaborating with a team, I contribute to defining the overall user experience. It's fulfilling to have the opportunity to design innovative experiences tailored to the unique needs of a diverse industry.

On a day-to-day basis, my responsibilities encompass various user-centric activities such as conducting user research, ideating and conceptualizing solutions, prototyping, creating high-fidelity designs and assets, and keeping up with the latest design trends in emerging digital environments.
